## Hi there 👋

🔬 Leach Lab

🌐 **Website:** https://nematostellalab.github.io

The Leach Lab studies behavior, circadian biology, and host–environment interactions in marine invertebrates, with a focus on *Nematostella vectensis*.

We combine experimental biology, low-cost engineering, and behavioral research to understand how organisms interact with their environment across time.

---

## 🧬 Research Focus

* Circadian rhythms and biological timing
* Behavior in marine invertebrates
* Host–environment interactions
* Low-cost and open scientific tools

---

## 🧪 Projects

This GitHub hosts tools, data, and experiments related to:

* Behavioral tracking and analysis
* Bioinformatics and data pipelines
* Experimental system design
* Open-source lab infrastructure
